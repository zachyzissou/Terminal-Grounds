# Terminal Grounds Art Bible

## Visual Direction

### Core Aesthetic
Terminal Grounds blends military realism with sci-fi extraction gameplay. The visual style emphasizes:

- **Grounded Realism**: Authentic military equipment and industrial environments
- **Sci-Fi Accents**: Alien technology as distinct visual elements without overwhelming the core aesthetic  
- **Post-Apocalyptic Grit**: Weathered, salvaged, jury-rigged equipment showing signs of the cascade event
- **Faction Identity**: Clear visual distinction between the seven major factions

## Silhouette Design Language

### Vehicle Silhouettes

## Material Motifs

### Human Technology Materials
- **Base Materials**: Mil-spec aluminum, carbon steel, polymer composites
- **Surface Treatments**: Anodization, parkerizing, cerakote coatings
- **Wear Characteristics**: Predictable weathering, contact wear, corrosion
- **Detail Elements**: Rivets, welds, threaded fasteners, data plates

### Hybrid Technology Materials
- **Base Materials**: Alien alloys, energy-conducting ceramics, adaptive polymers
- **Surface Treatments**: Energy etching, thermal conditioning, field hardening
- **Wear Characteristics**: Heat stress patterns, energy burn marks, material fatigue
- **Detail Elements**: Energy conduits, heat exchangers, field generators

### Alien Technology Materials
- **Base Materials**: Metamaterial composites, energy-matter constructs, living metal
- **Surface Treatments**: Quantum surface states, phase-locked structures
- **Wear Characteristics**: Reality distortions, phase shifts, temporal wear
- **Detail Elements**: Impossible joints, flowing surfaces, self-repair systems

## Lighting Bible

### Day Lighting
- **Key Light**: Sun angle 45°, warm white (5600K), intensity 8.0
- **Fill Light**: Sky dome, cool blue (6500K), intensity 2.0
- **Rim Light**: Atmospheric scatter, warm (5200K), intensity 1.5
- **Color Grading**: Slight desaturation, increased contrast

### Dusk Lighting
- **Key Light**: Sun angle 15°, warm orange (3200K), intensity 6.0
- **Fill Light**: Twilight blue (8000K), intensity 1.5
- **Rim Light**: Atmosphere glow, amber (2800K), intensity 2.0
- **Color Grading**: Enhanced warmth, increased saturation

### Night Lighting
- **Key Light**: Primary artificial sources, cool white (4000K), intensity 4.0
- **Fill Light**: Ambient reflection, blue-gray (4500K), intensity 0.8
- **Rim Light**: Secondary sources, varied temperatures, intensity 1.0
- **Color Grading**: Cooler tones, deeper shadows

### Storm Lighting
- **Key Light**: Overcast diffuse, neutral (5000K), intensity 3.0
- **Fill Light**: Storm scatter, gray-blue (6000K), intensity 1.0
- **Rim Light**: Lightning flashes, pure white (6500K), intensity 8.0 (momentary)
- **Color Grading**: Desaturated, high contrast

### EMI (Electromagnetic Interference) Lighting
- **Key Light**: Reduced natural, flickering (various), intensity variable
- **Fill Light**: EM glow, purple-blue (8500K), intensity 1.5
- **Rim Light**: Energy arcs, cyan-white (9000K), intensity 3.0
- **Color Grading**: Heavy color shift, electronic interference artifacts

## UI Style Guide

### Typography
- **Primary Font**: Military stencil fonts for readability
- **Secondary Font**: Clean sans-serif for data display
- **Size Hierarchy**: Large (alerts), Medium (primary info), Small (details)

### Iconography
- **Style**: Minimalist, high contrast symbols
- **Categories**: Status, warning, faction, equipment, mission
- **Color Coding**: Green (good), Yellow (caution), Red (danger), Blue (info)

### HUD Elements
- **Heat/Charge Bars**: Progressive fill with color-coded thresholds
- **Compass**: Diegetic design integrated with helmet systems
- **Crosshairs**: Weapon-specific, faction-themed variations

### Diegetic Interface Design
- **Primary Elements**: Helmet HUD overlays, weapon display systems
- **Animation**: Subtle scan lines, data refresh cycles, system boot sequences
- **Integration**: Heat, charge, EMP, and jam indicators as helmet overlay elements

## Master Material Requirements

### Layered PBR + RVT
- **Base Layer**: Albedo, Normal, Roughness, Metallic, AO
- **Detail Layer**: Micro-surface variation, wear patterns
- **Damage Layer**: Battle damage, weathering effects
- **Faction Layer**: Color tinting, emblem application

### Surface Conditions
- **Wet**: Reflection enhancement, normal map variation
- **Dust**: Albedo darkening, roughness increase, particle accumulation
- **Snow**: Albedo lightening, displacement mapping
- **Blood**: Albedo staining, wet surface properties
- **Radiation Sheen**: Emissive enhancement, color shift

### Emissive Channels
- **Hybrid Technology**: Energy conduits, heat vents, power cores
- **Alien Technology**: Phase fields, quantum fluctuations, living materials
- **Niagara Sprite-Lighting Hooks**: Energy effects, particle system integration

## LUT (Look-Up Table) Requirements

### Mood-Based LUTs
- **Standard**: Balanced color, slight desaturation for realism
- **Combat**: Increased contrast, enhanced reds and oranges
- **Stealth**: Cooler tones, reduced saturation, enhanced blues
- **Salvage**: Warmer tones, increased browns and golds
- **Tech**: Enhanced cyans and purples, sci-fi color grading

### Weather LUTs
- **Clear**: Standard color balance, full saturation
- **Overcast**: Reduced contrast, cooler color temperature  
- **Storm**: High contrast, dramatic shadows, cool highlights
- **Dust**: Warm color shift, reduced visibility, amber tinting
- **Rain**: Enhanced reflections, cooler tones, increased contrast

## Faction Emblems

### SVG Requirements
Each faction requires scalable vector graphics for:
- **Primary Emblem**: Main faction identifier, full detail
- **Simplified Icon**: Reduced detail for small scale display
- **Badge Variant**: Circular format for UI elements
- **Banner Version**: Horizontal format for wide displays

### Technical Specifications
- **Format**: SVG with embedded color information
- **Resolution**: Scalable from 16px to 2048px
- **Color Variants**: Full color, monochrome, faction-tinted versions
- **Usage Rights**: Faction customization, player identification, UI integration
